NX Cabinets

Quality wood cabinets for the Canadian market.

Our goal is to establish long-term relationships with Property Developers, Property Managers, and Contractors by providing quality cabinets for your properties and projects.

With our strategic global manufacturers and suppliers, we’re pleased to provide you with a selection of premium engineered cabinets at high volume savings. A vast majority of our NX Cabinet line is currently warehoused in BC where we typically ship to all major cities in Western Canada within 5 to 7 business days.

Why choose NX Cabinets?

RTA (Ready To Assemble) cabinets are an option that is intended to help speed up your construction schedule while providing premium quality wood cabinets for your kitchen and bathroom design. With our vast selection of readily available designed cabinet sizes, we can provide you a custom look without the high price. 

  • Premium quality control – All our cabinetry products are 100% Carb2 compliant.
  • Inventory Security – Our goal is to prepare your products within 24 hours after confirming orders.
  • Personal Service – We provide support for any questions about installation.
  • Reliable Shipping – We typically send your orders within 24 hours after confirmation.
